About Me

My Journey into Web Development

Hi! I’m Tosia, a former IT engineer with a background in teleinformatics, currently navigating the world of web development — and motherhood. I used to design and implement large-scale systems for monitoring network infrastructure, and I loved the mix of logic, planning, and problem-solving. Now, as I take care of my 4-month-old daughter, I’ve found a new spark: frontend development.

What started as simple curiosity (and a bit of jealousy over my husband’s JavaScript skills!) quickly turned into a full-on passion. I’m currently working through Dr. Angela Yu’s Web Development Bootcamp and discovering how exciting it is to build things from scratch — to see ideas come to life in the browser.

In my free time, I love playing board games, and I dream of one day creating my own — maybe even a web-based version. For me, coding is more than just a career shift. It’s a creative outlet, a mental challenge, and a space where I can grow at my own pace.

I’m excited to share my journey with you and hopefully inspire others to explore the world of web development. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or just starting out, I believe there’s always something new to learn and create. Let’s embark on this adventure together!
